Rules & How It Works

🏁 Event Format

Each competitive heat starts with participants at the base of the slide. They race up and slide down as fast as they can. Top finishers advance to semi-finals and then finals.

🎲 How Betting Works

  • All betting is parimutuel – winners split the losers’ money, proportionally.
  • Odds are dynamic and update immediately as bets come in.
  • You can bet any amount. If your bet wins, your payout is based on how many others also picked that outcome.
  • There are no shorts, and you can't lose more than you bet.
  • If a competitor drops out, bets on them are refunded.
  • 3% of all wagers go to local charities.

📅 Markets

  • Who will advance from each heat
  • Who will win each overall competition
  • Prop bets (e.g. Nir vs. Greg, winning department)

📧 Eligibility

  • Bets must be placed using your work email.
  • No proxy betting – only bet for yourself.

💬 Chatter & Support

Please use the #meadow-madness Slack channel for all betting discussions and live updates. Betting will also be available in person at the event!
